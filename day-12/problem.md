# Day 12 — 2723. Add Two Promises

## Problem
Given two promises `promise1` and `promise2`, both resolving with numbers, return a new promise that resolves with the sum of their resolved values.

## Examples

### Example 1
promise1 → 2  
promise2 → 5  
Output: 7

### Example 2
promise1 → 10  
promise2 → -12  
Output: -2

## Constraints
- `promise1` and `promise2` resolve with numbers
